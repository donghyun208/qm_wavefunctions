1D Quantum Systems:


These demos are visualizations of one dimensional quantum systems. The horizontal axis is the x-axis, while the vertical and out-of-plane axes are the real and imaginary components of the wavefunction.

You can click and drag the plots to get a better perspective of the complex plane. Try changing the initial phase and magnitude of the eigenstates to explore how the coefficients change the wavefunction.
