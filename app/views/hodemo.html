The quantum harmonic oscillator is a particle subject to a harmonic potential $V(x) = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$. It is often convenient to write this potential in terms of the angular frequency $(\omega)$, which is defined as $\omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}$.
$$ \begin{align*} \hat{H} &= -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\frac{\mathrm{d}^2}{\mathrm{d}x^2} + \frac{1}{2}m\omega^2x^2 \end{align*} $$
